About Us

The Sarari Homestead is a rural farm dedicated to growing organic vegetables and producing fresh dairy under the brand Sukhmani Dairy Milk. We believe in clean, sustainable farming that nurtures both the land and our community.

Our Products

  • Fresh Organic Tomatoes
  • Leafy Greens (Spinach, Methi)
  • Sukhmani Dairy Milk (Full Cream)
  • Homemade Paneer

Why Choose Us?

We use no chemicals or pesticides. Our products are fresh, local, and naturally grown. Delivered directly from farm to your doorstep.
